About Green Valley Public School

Our Vision

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to become global citizens who can make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Mission

Green Valley Public School is committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and character development through innovative teaching methods and holistic education.

Mrs. Sarah Johnson

Principal, Green Valley Public School

"At Green Valley, we believe every child has unique potential. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to help students discover their strengths and develop into well-rounded individuals."

Our History

Founded in 1995, Green Valley Public School began as a small institution with just 120 students. Today, we proudly serve over 1,800 students across grades K-12 on our state-of-the-art 15-acre campus.

Our journey has been marked by consistent academic excellence, with our students regularly achieving top positions in board examinations and prestigious competitions.

Admission Process

Join Green Valley Public School

Admissions are open for the academic year 2023-24. We welcome students from kindergarten to grade 12.

Eligibility Criteria

Pre-School (Nursery, KG)

Child must be 3+ years for Nursery and 4+ for KG as of April 1, 2023.

Classes I-VIII

Admission based on previous academic performance and interaction.

Class IX

Entrance test in English, Math, and Science.

Class XI

Based on Class X board results and stream selection.

Admission Process Step-by-Step

1
Inquiry

Visit school website or call us for prospectus and details.

2
Application

Fill out the admission form along with required documents.

3
Assessment

Child interaction/entrance test (where applicable).

4
Approval

Admission committee reviews application and assessment.

5
Fee Payment

Complete admission by paying fees within deadline.
